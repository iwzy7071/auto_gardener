# Code of Conduct

Gardener aims to be a practical, respectful open-source project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but be clear when something is unsafe or incorrect.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give actionable feedback and accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or exploit details in public project channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its users. Security-sensitive content may be removed immediately to protect users.
